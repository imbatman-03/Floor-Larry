Floor Larry

Floor Larry is a unique interactive gallery where every artwork tells its own story. Visitors can explore each piece, tap the titles to reveal their narratives, and immerse themselves in the world of creativity and imagination curated on this floor.

Super Larry
Super Larry stands as the fearless guardian of this floor, radiating strength and courage. His presence commands attention and inspires those who step into his domain.
Spider Larry
Spider Larry weaves intricate patterns of imagination across the walls. His delicate artistry hides complex stories, waiting for the curious to discover them.
Arthur Larry
Arthur Larry is the wise observer, blending mystery with elegance. Every glance invites reflection, revealing layers of hidden meaning in his gaze.
